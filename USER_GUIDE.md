# HireSense - User Guide

> India's AI-Powered Freelancing Platform | 100% Free

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Visit the Platform
Go to **https://free-lancer-api.vercel.app/**

You'll land on the homepage showing:
- **"Post a Gig"** button (for employers)
- **"Find Work"** button (for freelancers)
- Stats: 2,800+ freelancers, 500+ gigs, 80+ skills, ₹0 platform fee
- "How It Works" section (3 steps)
- Language selector (5 languages)

---

### Step 2: Create an Account
Click **"Sign Up"** (top-right corner)

**Options:**
- **Email + Password** — Enter email and password (min 6 characters)
- **Google** — One-click Google sign-in

Already have an account? Click **"Log In"** instead.

---

### Step 3: Choose Your Role (Onboarding)
After signing up, you'll see: **"Let's Get You Started"**

Pick one:
| Role | Who is it for? |
|------|---------------|
| **I'm a Freelancer** | You want to find work and get paid for your skills |
| **I'm an Employer** | You want to hire talented freelancers for your projects |

Click **Continue** to proceed.

## For Freelancers

### Browse Available Gigs
**Where:** Click **"Find Work"** on homepage, or go to `/gigs`

Each gig card shows:
- **Title** — e.g., "Build a React Dashboard for SaaS Analytics Platform"
- **Company** — Who posted it (e.g., TechCorp India)
- **Date & Location** — When posted + Remote/City
- **Budget** — e.g., ₹15,000 - ₹30,000 (fixed or hourly)
- **Bid count** — How many freelancers have already bid
- **Skill tags** — e.g., React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS

**Filters available:**
- Search box (type keywords)
- Category dropdown (All Categories, Web Development, Design, etc.)

---

### View Gig Details
**Where:** Click on any gig card

The detail page shows:
- **Full description** — Requirements, nice-to-haves
- **Budget range** — Fixed price or hourly rate
- **Duration** — e.g., 2-3 weeks
- **Experience level** — Beginner / Intermediate / Expert
- **Number of proposals** — How many bids so far
- **Location** — Remote or specific city
- **Deadline** — Last date to apply
- **About the Client** — Company name, rating, gigs posted
- **Existing bids** — Shows bidders with their:
  - Name, rating, gigs done
  - Bid amount and delivery time
  - **AI Match Score** (green badge, e.g., "AI Match Score: 92%")

Click **"Place a Bid"** at the bottom to submit your proposal.

---

### Place a Bid
**Where:** Click "Place a Bid" on any gig detail page (must be logged in)

Fill in:
- **Bid amount** (in ₹)
- **Delivery days** — How many days you need
- **Cover letter** — Why you're the best fit

The system will automatically calculate your **AI Match Score** based on your skills and the gig requirements.

---

### Your Dashboard (After Login)
**Where:** Click **"Dashboard"** in navbar → Sidebar navigation

| Sidebar Menu | What's There |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview — active gigs, pending bids, earnings summary |
| **Profile** | Edit your display name, headline, bio, hourly rate, city, skills, UPI/bank details, portfolio links (GitHub, Behance, LinkedIn, Dribbble) |
| **Bids** | Track all your submitted bids — Pending / Accepted / Rejected |
| **Invoices** | View and download invoice PDFs for completed work |
| **Portfolio** | Manage portfolio items — sync GitHub repos automatically, add projects manually, upload video pitch |
| **Reviews** | See reviews received, your Trust Score (0-100 gauge), individual ratings |
| **Messages** | Chat inbox — real-time messaging with clients |
| **Analytics** | Earnings chart, bid success rate, profile views, skill gap analysis |

---

### Messages / Chat
**Where:** Click **"Messages"** in sidebar or navbar

- Left panel: **Conversation list** — shows contact name, gig title, last message, timestamp, unread count (blue badge)
- Right panel: **Chat window** — select a conversation to start chatting
- Real-time messaging (messages appear instantly)
- File attachments supported

---

### Portfolio
**Where:** Freelancer Dashboard → Portfolio

Features:
- **Sync GitHub** — Automatically imports your public repos (shows stars, forks, language)
- **Add Manual** — Add projects from Behance, Dribbble, or any URL
- **Video Pitch** — Record a 2-minute video introduction using your browser camera
- **Skill Badges** — Verified badges (GitHub verified, assessment passed, portfolio verified)

---

### Invoices
**Where:** Freelancer Dashboard → Invoices

Each invoice shows:
- Invoice number (format: BW-2026-NNNN)
- Gig name and client
- Milestone line items with amounts
- Subtotal, Platform Fee (10%), GST (18%), **Total in ₹**
- Your payment details (UPI ID or bank account)
- **Download as PDF** button

---

### Analytics
**Where:** Freelancer Dashboard → Analytics

See:
- **Earnings chart** — Monthly earnings over time
- **Bid success rate** — % of bids accepted
- **Profile views** — How many employers viewed your profile
- **Skill Gap Analysis** — AI suggests missing in-demand skills with job match count
- **Referral Card** — Share your referral link via WhatsApp or X (Twitter)

## For Employers

### Post a New Gig
**Where:** Click **"Post a Gig"** on homepage, or Dashboard → Gigs → "New Gig"

Fill in:
- **Title** (5-100 characters)
- **Description** (20-5000 characters)
- **Category & Subcategory**
- **Budget** — Min/Max in ₹ (minimum ₹500), Fixed or Hourly
- **Duration** — How long the project takes
- **Experience Level** — Beginner / Intermediate / Expert
- **Deadline** — When you need it done
- **Remote?** — Toggle on/off
- **City & State** (if not remote)
- **Required Skills** — Pick 1-10 skills from 80+ options

**Voice-to-Task:** Click the mic icon to describe your gig by voice (supports Hindi-English mixed speech). AI will auto-fill the form.

---

### Review Bids
**Where:** Dashboard → Gigs → Click a gig → View bids

Each bid shows:
- Freelancer name, avatar, rating, gigs completed
- Bid amount and delivery time
- **AI Match Score** — How well the freelancer matches your gig (0-100%)
- Cover letter

Actions:
- **Accept Bid** — Starts the project, creates milestones
- **Reject Bid** — Decline the proposal

---

### Your Dashboard (After Login)
**Where:** Click **"Dashboard"** in navbar → Sidebar navigation

| Sidebar Menu | What's There |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview — active gigs, pending bids, total spent |
| **Profile** | Edit company name, bio, website, industry |
| **Gigs** | All your posted gigs — Draft / Open / In Progress / Completed |
| **Gigs → New** | Post a new gig (form + voice input) |
| **Invoices** | View and download invoices for hired freelancers |
| **Messages** | Chat with freelancers |
| **Disputes** | Flag and manage disputed gigs |
| **Analytics** | Spending chart, talent heatmap (by city & skill), GMV |

---

### Milestones
**Where:** Open an active gig → Milestones tab

Workflow:
1. **Employer creates milestones** when accepting a bid (e.g., "Design mockups - ₹5,000")
2. **Freelancer submits deliverable** for each milestone (upload files)
3. **Employer approves** each milestone
4. **Invoice generated** automatically after all milestones approved

Status flow: Pending → In Progress → Submitted → Approved

---

### Invoices & Payment
**Where:** Dashboard → Invoices

- Invoices are **auto-generated** after milestone approval
- Download as PDF — shows breakdown with GST
- **Payment is manual** — Pay freelancer via UPI or bank transfer (details on invoice)
- Mark invoice as "Paid" after you transfer the money

## Quick Flows

### Freelancer: Find Work & Get Paid
```
Sign Up → Choose "Freelancer" → Complete Profile → Browse Gigs →
Place Bid → Bid Accepted → Work on Milestones → Submit Deliverables →
Milestones Approved → Invoice Generated → Receive Payment (UPI/Bank)
```

### Employer: Hire & Pay
```
Sign Up → Choose "Employer" → Complete Profile → Post a Gig →
Review Bids (see AI Match Scores) → Accept Best Bid →
Track Milestones → Approve Deliverables → Download Invoice PDF →
Pay via UPI/Bank → Mark as Paid
```
